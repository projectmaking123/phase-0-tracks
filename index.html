The Art of GO

Thousands of years of strategy

Source: Fcb981 / Wikimedia Commons

A game that has been dated as far back as 4000 years, still played today in its original design. A simple composition of two sides. Every stone equal in value. There is no race to capture a king, but rather a race to seek balance.

Every move in GO must be made with the intentions of both defending and attacking. To maintain as many options as one can forsee.

Below is the rank system of strength, where 30 Kyu is the base and 9 Dan is the strongest.

  • 30 kyu to 1 kyu
  • 1 Dan to 9 Dan
